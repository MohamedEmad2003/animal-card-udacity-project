Lion

Lion is a predatory animal belonging to the mammalian mammal species, and is now classified as an endangered species.

  • Scientific Name  : Panthera leo
  • Habitat  : Africa
  • Average lifespan  : 10-14 years
  • speed  : 80 km\h

The lion is the king of the jungle and this title is his primary title because it is one of the strongest animals in the forest . It is characterized by thick hair at the shoulder area and has sharp canines, and is characterized by strength and hardness .
